Our Vision

To become a well-known global chain of Thai street food restaurants, offering original and authentic flavors that bring joy and satisfaction to customers worldwide.

Our Mission

  • Deliver authentic Thai street food with rich flavors and fresh ingredients.
  • Promote sustainable practices and eco-friendly sourcing.
  • Empower local communities by creating jobs and enhancing skills.
  • Provide exceptional customer service for an unforgettable dining experience.

History of NIASE Tomyam

At NIASE Tomyam Street Food was established in 2020 and is managed by Dania Sedek, a passionate entrepreneur dedicated to bringing authentic flavors to food lovers. The restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes, including their signature Tomyam Seafood, Nasi Khamook, Tom Saab, Daging Harimau Menangis, and other popular Thai street food. It's a must-visit destination for those seeking flavorful, spicy, and aromatic meals in a casual and welcoming atmosphere.
